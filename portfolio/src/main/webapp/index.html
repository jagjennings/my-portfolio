I am a rising Junior at Xavier University in New Orleans, majoring in Computer Science with a double minor in Math and Mandarin Chinese. I have participated in several Computer Science oriented summer programs, including Google’s Engineering Practicum Internship, Google's Computer Science Summer Institute and the University of California, Irvine’s ASPIRE program. I enjoy focusing on front-end development and this portfolio is a culmination of 5 weeks of webdev walkthroughs provided as part of my STEP Internship. I hope you like it!

I enjoy making 3-D art and ceramics, particularly wheel-throwing. I also love watching movies and TV shows and I'm always looking for recommendations on what to watch next (feel free to drop suggestions in the comments below)!
